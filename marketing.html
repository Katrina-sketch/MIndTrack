Marketing Strategy

MindTrack is designed for mental wellness and productivity. Our marketing plan includes:

  • Social media campaigns targeting wellness communities
  • Partnerships with schools and small organizations
  • App store optimization with clear descriptions and screenshots
  • Email newsletters to users highlighting features and updates
  • Blog posts and content marketing about healthy habits and productivity

By combining engaging content and strategic outreach, MindTrack aims to reach users who value mental wellness and personal organization.
